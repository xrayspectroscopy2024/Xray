The route from the airport

From Shanghai Hongqiao International Airport to Fengjing Hotel

  1. By taxi:

    2. 1、Please follow the sign TAXI.
    2、You can show the taxi drivers the address of the hotel in Chinese (the taxi driver may not speak English)
    枫憬酒店
    杨浦区殷行路1488号

    Spend: About 200RMB

    Time: About 40 minutes

  2. By subway:

    3. 1、Take line 10 at the subway station of the airport
    2、Get off at Xinjiangwancheng Station.
    3、 Go to Exit 7 and the hotel is at about 100 m.

    Spend: About 6RMB

    Time: About one hour and a half
